Objectives

In this stage, you should:

  • Add a list (unordered or ordered) with id "laps" to the body of the HTML.

  • Handle Reset Button Click Event:

    • After pressing the reset button, update the displayed time to "00:00:00".

    • Also, clear the list of laps.

  • Handle Lap Button Click Event:

    • After pressing the lap button, show the lapped times in the body.

    • Create a new list item (<li>) containing the lap time.

    • Append the new list item to the list (#laps) for displaying lap times.
